Frequently Asked Questions

  • I did not receive a confirmation email upon registration. Should I be worried?  Absolutely not! Don't worry, we keep good track of data :)

  • Where can I find the schedule?  We will send out emails to all registrants once we get confirmation from the department regarding timings.

  • Will I get certificates?  No! We are college communitites and are not registered brands to give away certificates. This is not a certification course.

  • Whom do I talk to if I have any queries?  Talk to your class coordinators and they'll know how to find us.

  • From whom do I seek help regarding community projects?  Every community has managers who will guide you if you have any issues. You can talk to teachers too.

  • I have a set of skills and would like to contribute to a community. How do I do that?  Again, talk to your class coordinators and they'll help you find us.
